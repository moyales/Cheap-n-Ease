Students often have trouble managing their limited funds. Many need to preserve what they have, and managing all potential debts can be time-consuming. This is when Cheap and Ease comes in. Cheap and Ease is an easy to use website that lets users keep track of their expenses. Our website provides various services for money-tight college students who want to enjoy their experience while not blowing their budget. We try to stay true to our motto as much as possible.


Our website is easy to use, accessible and free.
With Cheap & Ease, saving your money is a breeze!
